How to Make a Peanut Butter and Sugar sandwhich

  1. Gather ingredients
    1. Bread
    2. Peanut Butter
    3. Sugar
    4. Knife
    5. Plate
  2. Spread peanut butter
    1. Take out slice of bread
    2. Use the knife to spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. Spread Sugar
    1. grab a pinch or small scoop
    2. Sprinkle sugar on peanut butter
  4. fold bread in half, peanut butter side in
  5. Enjoy your sandwich!
